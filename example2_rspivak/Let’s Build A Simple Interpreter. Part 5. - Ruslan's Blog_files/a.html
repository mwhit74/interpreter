                                            rspivak Mod Ronnie Holm • 5 years ago • edited

                                            Hi Ronnie,

                                            Thank you for reading and thanks for the great question.
                                            If I had to add the power operator I would update the grammar to have one more rule to handle the highest precedence level in the table for the power operator **.

                                            The modified precedence table would look like this:

                                            level   operators
                                            2          +, -
                                            1          *, /
                                            0          **

                                            And the update to the grammar would look like this.

                                            Before:

                                                    expr   : term ((PLUS | MINUS) term)*
                                                    term   : factor ((MUL | DIV) factor)*
                                                    factor : INTEGER | LPAREN expr RPAREN

                                            After:

                                                    expr   : term ((PLUS | MINUS) term)*
                                                    term   : factor ((MUL | DIV) factor)*
                                                    factor : atom (POW factor)*
                                                    atom   : INTEGER | LPAREN expr RPAREN

                                            I hope that helps.
